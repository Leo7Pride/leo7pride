Raichur Pilgrimage Tour Highlights

  • 🚆 Journey from Mumbai to Raichur (Train)
  • 🏨 Comfortable stay at a Raichur hotel
  • 🙏 Darshan at Urukunda, Panchmukhi, and Mantralaya
  • 🛕 Visit to Kuruvapur Shri Dattatreya Temple, Balaji Temple & Surya Temple
  • 🎁 Memorable spiritual experience

Itinerary

🚆

Day 1: Journey to Raichur

  • Depart from Mumbai via Train
🚖

Day 2: Arrival in Raichur

  • Pickup from Raichur railway station
  • Transfer to hotel and breakfast
  • Proceed to Urukunda, Panchmukhi, and Mantralaya
  • Return to hotel for dinner and overnight stay
🙏

Day 3: Temple Visits

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Proceed to Kuruvapur Shri Dattatreya Temple, Balaji Temple, and Surya Temple
  • Return to hotel for rest and dinner
🛕

Day 4: Departure

  • Check out from the hotel
  • Drop off at Raichur railway station for return journey to Mumbai
