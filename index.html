know about some places before your travel

featured places

Taj Mahal, Agra

A symbol of love, the Taj Mahal is one of the Seven Wonders of the World

Jaipur (Rajasthan)

Known as the "Pink City," Jaipur boasts beautiful palaces, forts, and vibrant markets

Kerala Backwaters (Kerala)

Kerala’s backwaters offer a serene and unique experience with houseboat cruises on lagoons and lakes.

Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh)

One of the oldest living cities in the world, Varanasi is a spiritual and cultural hub on the banks of the Ganges River.

Leh-Ladakh (Jammu and Kashmir)

Known for its stunning landscapes, monasteries, and adventure activities like trekking, biking, and river rafting, Leh-Ladakh is a must-visit for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Mysore (Karnataka)

Known for its royal history and majestic Mysore Palace, Mysore is also famous for its traditional silk, sandalwood, and the grand Dasara Festival.
