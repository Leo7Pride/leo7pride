Heritage & Cultural Tour

Golden Triangle Tour (Delhi - Agra - Jaipur) of 7 Days

Tour Description

Experience the rich heritage and vibrant culture of India with our comprehensive tour package. Visit the iconic cities of Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur, and explore their historical landmarks, local cuisine, and cultural experiences.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival in Delhi
    Arrival at Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi. Transfer to your hotel, check-in, and rest. In the evening, visit India Gate for a relaxing walk, and enjoy the view of the monument illuminated at night. Overnight stay in Delhi.
  • Day 2: Delhi Sightseeing
    Morning: Visit Red Fort (UNESCO World Heritage Site), explore the historical monument, its museums, and gardens. Visit Jama Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India. Lunch at a local restaurant. Afternoon: Explore Qutub Minar (UNESCO World Heritage Site), a symbol of Delhi's past. Visit the Lotus Temple (Baha'i House of Worship) for a peaceful retreat. Evening: Enjoy a cultural show or dinner at Dilli Haat, a vibrant marketplace with handicrafts, food, and performances. Overnight stay in Delhi.
  • Day 3: Delhi to Agra (Approx. 3-4 hours drive)
    Morning: After breakfast, depart for Agra. Upon arrival, check-in to your hotel. Afternoon: Visit the magnificent Taj Mahal (UNESCO World Heritage Site) ‚Äî one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visit Agra Fort, a stunning example of Mughal architecture, and explore its rich history. Evening: Explore the local markets of Agra, famous for marble inlay work and leather goods. Overnight stay in Agra.
  • Day 4: Agra to Jaipur (Approx. 5-6 hours drive via Fatehpur Sikri)
    Morning: After breakfast, check-out from your hotel and depart for Jaipur. En route, stop at Fatehpur Sikri (UNESCO World Heritage Site) to explore the abandoned Mughal city, including Buland Darwaza and Jama Masjid. Continue your drive to Jaipur and check-in to your hotel. Evening: Visit Chokhi Dhani, an ethnic village resort offering traditional Rajasthani cultural experiences, including folk dances, music, and a Rajasthani meal. Overnight stay in Jaipur.
  • Day 5: Jaipur Sightseeing
    Morning: Visit the Amber Fort, a majestic hilltop fort with intricate architecture, and enjoy an elephant ride (optional). Stop at Jal Mahal, a beautiful palace in the middle of Man Sagar Lake. Lunch at a local restaurant. Afternoon: Visit City Palace, a splendid blend of Mughal and Rajput architecture, and explore its museums and courtyards. Visit Jantar Mantar (UNESCO World Heritage Site), an astronomical observatory with giant instruments. Evening: Explore the Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds) and enjoy the view of the Pink City from the top. Overnight stay in Jaipur.
  • Day 6: Jaipur to Delhi (Approx. 5-6 hours drive)
    Morning: After breakfast, check-out from your hotel and drive back to Delhi. Upon arrival, check-in to your hotel. Afternoon: Visit Humayun's Tomb (UNESCO World Heritage Site), a precursor to the Taj Mahal with beautiful gardens. Visit Lodhi Gardens for a peaceful walk amidst historical tombs and lush greenery. Evening: Explore Connaught Place for shopping, dining, and experiencing the vibrant city life. Overnight stay in Delhi.
  • Day 7: Departure from Delhi
    Morning: Depending on your flight timing, you can visit any last-minute attractions or relax. Transfer to Indira Gandhi International Airport for your departure.
