Shirdi & Shani Shingnapur Pilgrimage Tour

Join us on our INAUGURAL TOUR and experience the divine bliss of Shirdi!

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the sacred Shirdi Sai Baba Temple
  • Darshan at Shani Shingnapur
  • Separate rooms for male and female participants
  • VIP Pass for lunch at Sai Baba Bhojnalaya
  • Surprise gift for the first 50 customers!

Itinerary

Day 1: Night Journey (25th April)

  • Depart from Mulund in the evening
  • Comfortable night journey to Shirdi
  • Mineral water bottles provided for all participants

Day 2: Shirdi Darshan (26th April)

  • 06:00 AM – Arrive at Shirdi
  • 06:15 AM - 07:00 AM – Separate rooms for male and female participants for refreshments
  • 07:30 AM – Proceed to Sai Baba Darshan token counter and collect tokens
  • 08:00 AM - 11:30 AM – Join the queue for Sai Baba Darshan
  • 12:30 PM – Assemble at the parking point
  • 12:30 PM - 02:30 PM – Lunch at Sai Baba Bhojnalaya (VIP Pass provided)
  • 02:30 PM – Reassemble at the parking point

Shani Shingnapur Darshan

  • 02:45 PM – Depart for Shani Shingnapur
  • 04:30 PM – Arrive at Shani Shingnapur
  • 04:45 PM - 06:30 PM – Darshan at Shani Shingnapur
  • 06:30 PM – Tea refreshments with Tea and Biscuits/Toast

Return Journey to Mumbai

  • 08:00 PM – Depart for Mumbai
  • 09:30 PM – Dinner will be provided during the journey
  • Early Morning Arrival (27th April) – Reach Mumbai with divine blessings
